About Us

Our Story

Chiya Guff was founded in 2020 and continues to grow from strength to strength. Our goal is to pioneer a world of the highest quality loose leaf teas and infusions while helping our customers understand the origins, health benefits and flavors behind our finest selection of teas. To us, tea is an opportunity. To excite the senses. To surprise your taste buds. With a diverse range of bold flavors and bright colors. Everything we do starts with three main objectives – Pure Joy, Clean Life, Inspiring Tea.

- Rohit Ale (founder)
